About Bright Omasoro

Bright Omasoro is an accomplished Financial Analyst with over 2 years of experience in analyzing financial performance, developing financial models, and providing strategic insights to drive business performance. He has a strong background in financial planning and analysis (FP&A), with proven skills in preparing and presenting comprehensive financial reports, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and leveraging data-driven insights to support decision-making. Bright has worked with several organizations, including 10Analytics, Tech Drive Limited, and IHS Towers LTD, where he developed a deep understanding of financial analysis, budgeting, and forecasting. He is proficient in financial software like Dynamic GP, NAV 365(AX), and Tableau, and has advanced skills in Excel and SQL for financial modeling and reporting. His technical expertise is complemented by strong communication skills, enabling him to articulate financial insights and their implications to senior management and stakeholders. Bright is also committed to continuous improvement, consistently enhancing financial processes and reporting systems to increase accuracy and efficiency. With a Bachelor's degree in Accounting and Financial Management from the University of Sunderland and professional membership with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN), Bright is well-equipped to contribute to the financial success of any organization.
